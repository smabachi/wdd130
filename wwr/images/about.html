Whitewater Rafting Website Planning Document

Purpose

To create a clear, organized, and engaging online presence that promotes adventure packages, provides essential trip details, enables easy booking, and builds trust with customers through safety information, photos, and testimonials—ultimately helping grow the rafting business.

Goals

  • Promote Adventure Packages: Showcase half-day, full-day, multi-day, and group trips with descriptions, pricing, and images.
  • Enable Easy Online Booking: Allow users to securely book trips online.
  • Build Trust Through Safety Information: Present our top-tier safety practices for a secure adventure.
  • Educate Visitors: Provide clear details on adventures, pricing, safety, and guidelines.
  • Enhance Brand Visibility: Use blogs, social media, and galleries to connect with customers.
